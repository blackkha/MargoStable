About the Crypto Leverage Indicator Tracker

What is the Leverage Indicator?

The Leverage Indicator is a metric we've developed to identify cryptocurrencies with high trading activity relative to their market capitalization. While not a direct measure of leverage, it serves as a proxy for identifying assets that may be experiencing significant leverage trading.

How is it Calculated?

Our Leverage Indicator is calculated using the following formula: 

Leverage Indicator = (Volume / Market Cap) × (1 + |24h Price Change %| / 100) × 100
This formula considers:
  • Volume to Market Cap Ratio: A higher ratio suggests more trading activity relative to the asset's size
  • Price Volatility: We factor in 24-hour price changes as volatile assets often attract leverage traders

Data Sources

  • CCXT Library: As a fallback for exchange-specific data when needed
